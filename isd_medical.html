Items

  • Field Dressing Bandages : Dont use.
     
  • Elastic Bandage : The fastest and most effective bandage in terms of sealing wounds. Saturates and opens up in 5-6min.
     
  • Packing bandages : Extremely effective against avulsions. Saturates and opens up in 10-12min.
     
  • QuikClot bandages : Great for long term use as it may not seal quickly, but takes about 30min to saturate and open.
     
  • Adenosine : Lowers heart rate by -7 to -35 bpm. Takes 15 seconds to work, stays in your system for 2 minutes.
     
  • Epinephrine : Raises heart rate by 10 to 50 bpm. Takes 10 seconds to work, stays in your system for 2 minutes. Will increase how often the game runs a check of patients with a heart rate waking up from unconsciousness by a factor of 5. (3 seconds instead of 15 seconds).
     
  • Morphine : Reduces pain significantly after 30 seconds. Reduces heart rate by -10 to -35 bpm. Will stay in your system for 30 minutes. Use only once per 30min otherwise causes hear tfaliure.
     
  • Blood : Allows oxygen to get to your body. Too much blood loss results in Cardiac Arrest.
     
  • Plasma : Can be used as a substitute for blood, and improves clotting.
     
  • Saline : Can be used as a substitute for blood.
     
  • Tourniquet : Blocks off the flow of blood to a limb to prevent bleeding. Will result in no pulse when checked, the complete block of all medications and fluids transfused to the limb and pain will be felt after a few minutes.
     
  • Surgical Kit : Used to stitch up wounds to prevent reopening. Takes about 5 seconds for every wound. Unlimited uses.
     
  • Splint : Used to treat a fractured limb. When used on an arm it will reduce weapon sway and when used on a leg it will increase walking speed.
     
  • Suture : Used to stitch wounds so they will not reopen. Limited uses.
     
  • PAK : Stitches and fixes up all bruises and blood loss on all limbs and can only be used if the person is stable(conscious and not bleeding).
