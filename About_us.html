| About us

Welcome to Indian Spearhead Division (ISD), we are an Indian Arma 3 MilSim community. We are a semi-casual unit dedicated to having fun rather than achieving 100% realism. We hope to create a welcoming, non-toxic and friendly community of users who enjoy playing the game together. ISD is the only public Indian Arma 3 Unit currently operating and was created to provide a space for players to play India-centric missions based on the Indian armed forces. It was created out of a need for missions at times that are comfortable to Indian players. Our missions are mainly fictional in nature. We carry out a multitude of mission types with members learning new skills every mission. We do not exclusively look for experienced players, but welcome everybody.
 
Operation timings : 21:00-23:00 on Saturdays & 15:30-18:30 on Sundays
 
Modpack : Custom modpack (64 gb)
