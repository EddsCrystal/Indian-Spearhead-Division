Items

  • Field Dressing Bandages : Normal, average, generally not recommended. Reopens much faster than packing so not good for quick bandaging, and higher chance of reopening than QuikClot so not good for delayed stitching.
     
  • Elastic Bandage : Highest efficiency, higher reopen chance, but very short reopening delay.
     
  • Packing bandages : Good all around, but have a high chance to reopen. However, they’ll hold for a while, Giving you a while to get to a medic. These are recommended for all non medics out in the field.
     
  • QuikClot bandages : Lowest efficiency, but lowest reopen chance, and longer reopening delay. Best for when stitching will be delayed. Won’t reopen soon or likely but has terrible efficiency.
     
  • Adenosine : Lowers heart rate by -7 to -35 bpm. Takes 15 seconds to work, stays in your system for 2 minutes.
     
  • Epinephrine : Raises heart rate by 10 to 50 bpm. Takes 10 seconds to work, stays in your system for 2 minutes. Will increase how often the game runs a check of patients with a heart rate waking up from unconsciousness by a factor of 5. (3 seconds instead of 15 seconds).
     
  • Morphine : Reduces pain significantly after 30 seconds. Reduces heart rate by -10 to -35 bpm. Will stay in your system for 30 minutes. Use only once per 30min otherwise causes hear tfaliure.
     
  • Blood : Primary way to restore someone’s blood pressure. Blood does a body good.
     
  • Plasma : Bring up the blood pressure.
     
  • Saline : Bring up the blood pressure..
     
  • Tourniquet : Used to prevent bleeding from a limb. However, you cannot check pulse or blood pressure, will cause pain after 2 minutes, and prevents movement of medication and fluids.
     
  • Surgical Kit : Used to stitch wounds so they will not reopen. 5 seconds for each wound type. Unlimited uses.
     
  • Splint : Used to treat a fractured limb. When used on an arm it will reduce weapon sway and when used on a leg it will increase walking speed.
     
  • Suture : Used to stitch wounds so they will not reopen. Limited uses.
     
  • PAK : Completely resets a person medically.
